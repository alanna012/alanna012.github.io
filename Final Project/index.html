An Expression of Me

"Art is the only way to run away without leaving home" - Twyla Tharp

Art is the one thing in the world that allows people to express themselves. It brings people together through museums, websites, and in home artwork. The colors, shapes, strokes, mediums, all mean something. Art is up to a persons own interpretation and you can learn a lot about a person through how they perceive a piece of art.

Without art the earth is just eh.
